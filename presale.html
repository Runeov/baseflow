Frequently Asked Questions

When will the SAI token sale launch?
We're finalizing regulatory compliance and smart contract audits. Waitlist members will be the first to know — typically 2–4 weeks before the public announcement.
What is SAI?
SAI is the utility token that powers the Baseflow solar-AI node network. It's used for node operations, AI compute access, staking, and network governance. SAI is not a security — it provides utility within the Baseflow ecosystem.
What blockchain will SAI be on?
SAI will launch on Polygon (EVM-compatible). This means low gas fees, fast transactions, and compatibility with MetaMask and other Ethereum wallets.
Is joining the waitlist free?
Yes, completely free. No payment or wallet connection is required. Just your email address to receive priority access when the sale goes live.
What benefits do waitlist members get?
Priority allocation (first access to buy SAI), the lowest price tier, exclusive development updates, and early access to the Baseflow community channels.
How does Baseflow generate revenue?
Each Baseflow node generates revenue from four streams: GPU compute rentals via the AI inference marketplace, crypto mining rewards, DeFi yield farming, and energy sell-back to the Thai grid. Solar power eliminates electricity costs, and Thailand's BOI grants 8 years of tax exemption.
