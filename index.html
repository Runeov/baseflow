Regulatory Framework

Legal & Regulatory

Baseflow is structured from day one for full Thai SEC and BOI compliance — giving BFW holders access to Thailand's 5-year crypto capital gains tax exemption.

Headquarters

Kingdom of Thailand — leveraging BOI promotional privileges, ASEAN FTA trade agreements, and the Thai SEC digital asset framework.

Regulatory Body

Thai Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) — Digital Asset Exchange + ICO Portal license targets.

BOI Category

Renewable Energy Infrastructure — Category A2. Qualifies for 8-year corporate income tax exemption and 0% import duty on all machinery.

Crypto Tax Status

5-year personal income tax exemption on BFW capital gains for holders trading on Thai SEC-licensed exchanges. Valid until December 2029.

Solar Incentive

THB 200,000 deduction per rooftop solar installation under the June 2025 Thai Cabinet energy incentive package. Valid through December 2028.

ASEAN-China FTA

Solar panels (HS Code 8541.40) imported from China to Thailand attract 0% duty under ACFTA. ACFTA 3.0 (Oct 2024) includes a green clause further reducing tariffs on solar equipment.
